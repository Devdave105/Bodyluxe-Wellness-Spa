Who We Are

Luxury Redefined.

Bodyluxe Wellness Spa is Port Harcourt's premier luxury wellness destination — a space built on the belief that true beauty begins with deep, intentional care. We combine clinical expertise with a luxurious sensory environment to deliver results that go far beyond the surface.

With two established locations and a team of certified therapists, we serve clients who expect the very best — and we consistently deliver it. Whether you come for a single session or become a regular client, the Bodyluxe experience is always personal, always premium.
